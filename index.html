How the intelligence works

Method, provenance, limitations and clinician decisions in one reviewable layer.

Current methods

Rule: pace-aware dose, tolerance, carryover and review-date logic.

Trend: recent-session change compared with the preceding sessions.

Structured summary: templated synthesis of the highest-ranked signal and its source records.

Inputs and provenance

Programme targets, session dose and tolerance, clinician-recorded outcomes and linked equipment. Calculations remain inside the browser.

Limitations

No trained prediction model, diagnosis, treatment recommendation or autonomous action. Sample records are synthetic and this public prototype must not contain directly identifying patient data.
